Basic Computer Course to thrive in the 4th Industrial Revolution

Join us at Jagannath University IT Society (JnUITS) as we launch our comprehensive Basic Computer Course designed to equip you with the essential skills needed to thrive in the 4th Industrial Revolution. This course is a must for anyone looking to enhance their digital literacy, whether you're a current student, an alumnus, or a professional seeking to stay competitive in a non-IT job.

Course Highlights:

- 4 Months of Hands-On Learning: Gain practical experience with over 60 hours of training.

- Computer Basics: Understand the fundamentals of computer operation.

- MS Office 365 & Google Workspace: Master these essential productivity tools

- Materials & Assignments: Reinforce your learning with our comprehensive materials and assignments.

- Bi-Weekly Support Days: Get your questions and problems solved with our dedicated support days.

- Exams (Mid + Final): Assess your progress and understanding through our mid-term and final exams.

- Certification from JnUITS: Earn a certificate upon completing the course, adding a valuable credential to your resume.

Registration Fees:

- General Students: 500 BDT

- JnUITS General Members: 300 BDT (Collect Form Link From Messenger)

- JnUITS Accelerator and Executive Members: Free
